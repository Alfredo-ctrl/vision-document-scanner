Computer vision utility

Turn a rough document photo into a clean scan.

The tool detects page edges, corrects perspective, and exports a high contrast image from one terminal command.

Edge detection

Canny edges and contour ranking locate the strongest page-like shape in the image.

Perspective correction

A four point transform straightens the page and crops away the background.

Readable output

Adaptive thresholding produces a crisp black and white image ready for storage or sharing.
